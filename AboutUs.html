About CeylonSpizee

Welcome to our startup, a premier exporter of Ceylon cinnamon based in Sri Lanka. We specialize in producing only organic products, meticulously harvested from our certified organic cinnamon farmer network. Our commitment to quality and sustainability is at the heart of everything we do. At our company, we believe in fostering long-term B2B partnerships with international companies. Rather than focusing on short-term profits, our goal is to build enduring relationships that benefit all parties involved. We are dedicated to promoting organic food trends by offering our high-quality organic cinnamon at conventional prices, making it accessible to a broader market. As part of our company policy, we exclusively deal in organic products. This commitment is driven by our desire to encourage the global shift towards organic food consumption. To support this mission, we do not impose a minimum order quantity, allowing us to meet our clients' specific needs, no matter the size of their order. We are particularly passionate about supporting startup companies in the Food and Beverage industry worldwide. We understand the challenges of growing a young business, and we are here to help you succeed. By partnering with us, you join a network of like-minded companies dedicated to sustainability and growth.
