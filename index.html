Why Us?

Importing Ceylon cinnamon from Sri Lanka offers unmatched quality and numerous benefits over cheaper alternatives like Cassia. Ceylon cinnamon, known as "true cinnamon," boasts a delicate flavor profile, higher levels of essential oils, and lower coumarin content compared to Cassia. Its superior taste, aroma, and health benefits make it highly sought after in international markets.

By choosing CeylonSpizee, you're not only indulging in premium cinnamon but also supporting sustainable sourcing practices and empowering local communities in Sri Lanka.
