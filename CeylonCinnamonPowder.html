Ceylon Cinnamon Powder

Ceylon Cinnamon Powder, also known as "True Cinnamon Powder," is made from finely ground Ceylon cinnamon bark. It offers a subtle and unique flavor profile, making it a preferred choice for both culinary and medicinal uses.

Applications:

  • **Culinary Uses:** Ideal for baking, cooking, and seasoning dishes.
  • **Beverages:** Perfect for spicing up teas, coffees, and smoothies.
  • **Health Benefits:** Supports digestive health, controls blood sugar levels, and has anti-inflammatory properties.
  • **Beauty:** Used in homemade skincare recipes.
