Ceylon Cinnamon Sticks

Ceylon Cinnamon Sticks, also known as "True Cinnamon," are derived from the bark of the Ceylon cinnamon tree. Known for their delicate flavor and aroma, these sticks are a versatile spice used in a variety of culinary applications.

Applications:

  • Culinary Uses: Ideal for baking, cooking, and brewing teas.
  • Aromatherapy: Used in potpourri and scent sachets.
  • Health Benefits: Supports digestive health and has anti-inflammatory properties.
  • Crafts: Popular in DIY projects and home decor.
