Ceylon Cinnamon Oil

Ceylon Cinnamon Oil is a premium essential oil extracted from the bark of organically grown Ceylon cinnamon trees in Sri Lanka. Known for its potent aroma and numerous health benefits, it is a versatile product used in various applications.

Applications:

  • Aromatherapy: Enhances relaxation and reduces stress.
  • Culinary Uses: Adds a warm, spicy flavor to dishes.
  • Cosmetics: Used in skincare for its antibacterial properties.
  • Health Benefits: Supports digestive health and boosts immunity.

FAQ

What is Ceylon Cinnamon Oil?

Ceylon Cinnamon Oil is an essential oil derived from the bark of the Ceylon cinnamon tree, known for its aromatic and therapeutic properties.

How do I use Ceylon Cinnamon Oil?

It can be used in aromatherapy, added to foods for flavor, and applied in skincare products for its antibacterial benefits.

Is it safe for consumption?

Yes, when used in appropriate amounts, it is safe for culinary use. Always follow recommended guidelines for usage.
